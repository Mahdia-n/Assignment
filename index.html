. شب در میان است، خدا مهربان است

Even in the middle of the night, God is kind.

Meaning: God is there for you even in the worst situations.

. دنیا با امید زنده است

The world is alive with hope.

Meaning: It is hope that makes life meaningful

از تو حرکت، از خدا برکت.

From you the initiative, from God the blessing.

Meaning: you have to start to have god's help.

آب اگر صد پاره گردد باز هم آشناست.

Even if divided in hundred parts, water always gathers again.

Meaning: The one who is for you, everywhere will be for with you.

صبر تلخ است اما میوه اش شیرین است

Patience is bitter, but its fruit is sweet.

Explanation: This proverb teaches that enduring hardship with patience will eventually lead to rewarding results, reflecting the Afghan belief in perseverance.
